Guidelines

STANDING DIRECTIVES

DIR_01

Valid physical college ID card is mandatory for entry and must be presented during registration verification.

DIR_02

Professional or smart-casual attire required throughout the event. Shorts and slippers are strictly prohibited.

DIR_03

Report to assigned venues 30 minutes before your scheduled event time for briefing and setup.

DIR_04

Zero tolerance policy for drugs, alcohol, weapons, or any form of substance abuse on campus premises.

DIR_05

All registration fees are non-refundable. Tickets cannot be transferred without prior written approval.

DIR_06

Judge decisions are final and binding. No appeals or disputes will be entertained post-announcement.

Violations may result in immediate disqualification without refund. The organizing committee reserves the right to amend rules as necessary.
